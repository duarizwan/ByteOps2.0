C — Clean & Minimal

Near-monochrome palette with max whitespace. Brand icons are the only colour on the page — they naturally draw the eye to the tools. The UI steps back completely and lets the content breathe.

Near-monochrome Brand icons = only colour Maximum whitespace Content-first Like: Claude.ai, ChatGPT, Arc
