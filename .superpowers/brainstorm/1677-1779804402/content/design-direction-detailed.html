C — Clean & Minimal

Near-monochrome palette — near-white backgrounds, near-black text and accents. Maximum whitespace, no decorative elements. Content breathes. The UI disappears and the work takes centre stage.

Near-monochrome Maximum whitespace Content-first Light + optional dark Inspired by: Claude.ai, ChatGPT, Arc
